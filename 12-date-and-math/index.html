JavaScript Date and Math

Date Object

Date Object allows working with dates and times in JavaScript.

MethodDescription
getFullYear()Get 4-digit year
getMonth()Get month (0-11)
getDate()Get day of month (1-31)
getDay()Get day of week (0-6)
getHours()Get hours (0-23)
getMinutes()Get minutes (0-59)
getSeconds()Get seconds (0-59)
getTime()Get milliseconds since 1970
